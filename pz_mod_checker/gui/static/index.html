How Bisect Works

1 Click Start Bisect — the tool saves your mod list and enables half your mods.
2 Launch PZ, then come back and click PZ Crashed or PZ Loaded OK.
3 Repeat until the culprit mod is found. Usually takes 7-8 rounds for 165 mods.
